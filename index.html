About Us

AliSend Ltd is a UK-based company specialising in money remittance services, established to deliver secure, reliable, and regulatory-compliant digital financial solutions that enable individuals to send money across borders with confidence and ease.

Operating from the United Kingdom, we are fully committed to complying with all applicable laws and regulatory requirements, including Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and Know Your Customer (KYC) obligations, as well as consumer and data protection regulations.

At AliSend, compliance and security are at the core of our business model. We utilise advanced technology systems for identity verification, transaction monitoring, and risk management to ensure the integrity of our services and the protection of our customers and their funds at all times.

Our Approach

We believe money transfers should be:

  • Clear and transparent, with no hidden fees
  • Fast and simple, without unnecessary complexity
  • Secure and aligned with the highest regulatory standards
  • Supported by effective monitoring and control systems

Our Vision

To build a globally trusted money remittance platform that combines technology, compliance, and user experience, setting a new standard for digital financial services.

Our Mission

To provide digital money transfer services that are:

  • Fully compliant with regulatory requirements
  • Reliable and scalable
  • Easy to use
  • Designed to meet the needs of customers worldwide

Our Values

  • Compliance First: Operating in full accordance with applicable laws and regulations
  • Security: Protecting customer funds and data as a top priority
  • Transparency: Clear fees and fair exchange rates
  • Responsibility: Ethical and legal responsibility towards our customers
